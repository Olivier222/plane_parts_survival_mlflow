##removal.csv file:

REMOVAL_DT: The date when a component was removed
INSTALL_DT: When a component was installed before the removal
REMOVED_BOOL: 1 - removed, 0 - currently installed
REPETITIVE_FAULT_QT: The total count of repetitive fault raised against the component's ATA chapter when it was active
AIRCRAFT_AGE: Age of an aircraft
MEL_QTY: Number of MEL (Minimum Equipment List) failures
DELAY_QTY: Delay time in minutes
TIME_SINCE_INSTALL_CYCLES: The accrued cycles between the installation and removal date 
TIME_SINCE_NEW_CYCLES: The total cycles since the first installation (when it was new).
